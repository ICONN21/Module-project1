Overview

Bukayo Ayoyinka Temidayo Saka (born 5 September 2001) is an English professional footballer who plays as a right winger for Premier League club Arsenal and the England national team. He is considered to be one of the best young players in the world.

Life

Bukayo Saka was born on the 5th of September 2001 in Ealing, Greater London to Yoruba Nigerian parents, Adenike and Yomi Saka

Career

Bukayo has playes his entire profesional career with Arsenal. As one of the best young talents in world football, Saka was recently signed to a new contract keeping him at the club until at least 2028. Additional milestones include:

  • 2019 UEFA Europa League Runner Up
  • Helped England to qualify fro the 2022 FIFA World Cup
  • Led Arsenal in assists in 2022
  • Arsenals top scorer in the 2021-2022 season

Awards

Saka has already won an FA Cup and two community shields in his short time at Arsenal. He has also picked up back to back Player of the Year awards.
